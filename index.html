Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much are driving lessons in Manchester?

Cruise'N'Pass offers pay-as-you-go lessons at £35 per hour (2-hour minimum) and discounted block packages starting from £320 for 10 hours. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

What areas in Greater Manchester do you cover?

We provide driving lessons across Greater Manchester, including Manchester city centre, Salford, Stockport, Bolton, Bury, Atherton, Bredbury, Chadderton, Cheetham Hill, Didsbury, Withington, Chorlton, Cheadle, Sale, Old Trafford and many surrounding neighbourhoods.

What is your pass rate?

Cruise'N'Pass has an outstanding 90%+ first-time pass rate, with DVSA-approved instructors dedicated to building your confidence and skill on real roads.

Do you offer intensive driving courses?

Yes, our Fast Track Pass (30 hours, £990) and Zero to Hero (40 hours, £1,320) packages are designed for learners who want to pass their test quickly and comprehensively.

How do I book a driving lesson?

You can book a lesson by calling 07916 155054, emailing cruisenpass@gmail.com, or using the booking form on our website. We offer flexible scheduling 7 days a week from 7am to 8pm.
